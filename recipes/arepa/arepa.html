Arepas

The arepas are a tasty and iconic Venezuelan dish.

    Here are the ingridients needed to make them:

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt

    To prepare them, you'll have to:

  1. Mix the flour with water until it gets homogeneous
  2. Turn it into a ball
  3. Flatten the ball
  4. Put it into a hot pan until it gets hard
  5. turn around from one side to another until both are hard to touch
  6. eat it nomnomnom

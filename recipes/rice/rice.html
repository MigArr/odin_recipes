Rice

Rice is soft, white and tasty

    Here are the ingridients needed to make it:

  • Ricebean
  • Salt
  • Onions or/and Pepper

    To prepare it, you'll need to:

  1. Cut onions and peppers
  2. Put them in a hot pot
  3. When done, add for 1 cup of rice two of water
  4. Wait until the water is boiling and almost not visible in the surface of the rice
  5. Put a lid on the cooking pot for 20 minutes
  6. Remove from heat and wait another 10 minutes
  7. Eat it right away
